Chess

Chess is a two-player board game played on a checkered board with 64 squares.Chess requires strategy, critical thinking, and foresight, and is enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels around the world.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their symbol (usually X or O) and The game ends in a tie if all the squares are filled without a winner. It is a popular game for children and can be played with just a pen and paper.

Connect four

Connect Four is a two-player strategy game where players take turns dropping colored disks into a vertical grid. The game ends in a tie if all the disks are used without a winner. Connect Four is a popular game for children and adults alike, and can be played with a physical game set or online.

Snakegame

Snake is a classic video game where the player controls a growing line or "snake" that moves around the screen. The snake must eat food, which causes it to grow longer, while avoiding hitting walls, obstacles, and the snake's own tail.

Play

MemoryGame

A memory game is a type of game where players are required to remember the location of matching pairs of cards or tiles. Memory games can be played with various themes and difficulty levels and are popular among children and adults alike.

Play

Tetris

Tetris is a classic puzzle video game where players must stack falling blocks of different shapes and sizes to create complete rows with no gaps. Once a row is completed, it disappears and the player earns points. The game ends when the stack of blocks reaches the top of the playfield.
